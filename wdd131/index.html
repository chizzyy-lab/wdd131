Lagos Nigeria

Nigeria's history is a long and complex one,
spanning from ancient civilizations to British colonization and eventual independence.
The region now known as Nigeria has been inhabited for thousands of years,
with the Nok civilization being one of the earliest known. Several pre-colonial empires and kingdoms,
such as Kanem-Bornu, Benin, and Oyo, flourished in the area.
European explorers and traders arrived in the 15th century,
leading to the start of the slave trade, particularly with the Yoruba and Benin peoples.
In the 19th century, Britain gradually expanded its influence,
eventually establishing the Colony and Protectorate of Nigeria in 1914 by merging the Southern and Northern Protectorates.
Nigeria gained independence on October 1, 1960, and became a republic in 1963.
